Welcome to Beginvesting! The goal of this project is to make a beginner friendly investing website using trustable resources. Everything on here is free for everyones usage, and I hope you enjoy learning about the stock market and other finance-related topics.










About the devloper: I am a highschool student who made this website as a way to help those who want to get started with investing but don't yet know where to begin. Everything on here is extracted from my own hand written notes. I am excited to share this knowledge with you as a user of this website because everyone needs a starting point, and I would love for this website to be the beginning of your journey.
Disclaimer: Everything on this website is for learning purposes only. Consult a financial advisor before making any investment decisions with real money.
